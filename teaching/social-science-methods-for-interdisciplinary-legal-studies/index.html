Introduction to R

Silje Synnøve Lyder Hermansen
2023-10-30

PhD level. University of Copenhagen. Faculty of Law. 2023

Welcome to our R workshop! The purpose of this workshop is to provide you with the basic knowledge you need to navigate R independently. You won’t become an expert, but you will have an insight into - and firsthand experience with - how R operates.

This is a workshop. It means that we will be actively working in R together throughout the course. Bring your laptop and get ready to write! Please install R (https://www.r-project.org/; the statistical program) and RStudio (https://www.rstudio.com/products/rstudio/; the user interface) before the workshop begins.
